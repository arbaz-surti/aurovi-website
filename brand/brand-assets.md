# Aurovi Brand Assets

## Logo mark
- `logo-mark.svg` — white circle + amber gap. Use on dark backgrounds (#1F1A14).
- `logo-mark-light.svg` — dark circle + amber gap. Use on light/cream backgrounds.

**Concept:** A near-complete circle (time, continuity) with a deliberate amber gap at the top — the freed time. Aurovi gives small business owners their time back.

## Brand voice
Warm, personal, human, story-driven. Premium feel without being cold or corporate. The brand exists because Arbaz's father couldn't grow his factory — buried in manual work. Aurovi gives that time back.
